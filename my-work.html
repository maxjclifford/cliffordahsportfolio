Food Time App

Food Time was an Android app I developed during the 3rd year project, K'Ching, in which different groups acted as companies, competing to ideate, build, and market multiple related products. My group's products related to the theme of limiting food waste, and the purpose of Food Time was to manage food by expiration date to ensure food could be consumed on time and not go to waste. This was my first time working with mobile app development, and I used Android Studio (primarily using Java and XML) for this project, Android's official integrated development environment (IDE). The app also featured a way to create printable calendars out of food items expiring the current week. So far, it's the largest project I've made with programming.

"Communist Test" Animation

This was developed with Adobe Animate, and was primarily reliant on JavaScript. During the 3rd year project Turning Points, learners would pick some sort of historical event to research and would create a museum exhibit based on the event. This game was based on the Red Scare of the 1950s, and featured a player who's about to become a special agent at the Department of Justice and is being given a screening test to uncover communist ties and beliefs. While the test itself wasn't real, it was based on real material from the US government.
